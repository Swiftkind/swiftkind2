INITIAL RESEARCH

User Interviews

We wanted to find out what are the things we needed to automate to make things easier, faster and fault proof development process. So we conducted user interviews to business owners, project managers, developers and designers.

Guerilla Research

11 people interviewed

15 minutes each

  1. What improvements do you think are needed for our current processes?

  2. What are the process you’d like to be automated?

  3. How will these automated (or not) processes make your daily routine easier?

  4. What integrations you wanted to have?

INITIAL RESEARCH

Discussions

Here are few findings we gathered from the respondents

Business Owner

I wanted to monitor all the things that are happening on all departments on demand

Project Manegers

I want to be able to know all the timely progress and issues to ensure we meet deadlines

Devs & Designers

We wanted to be able to submit reports using our team communications medium like slack

Employee #1

We wanted a platform where we request for leaves and adjust schedules

Employee #2

I wanted to be able to know and asses my overall performance

Employee #3

I’d like to be updated about the companies latest and upcoming events.
